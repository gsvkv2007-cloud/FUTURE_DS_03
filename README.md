# FUTURE_DS_03
# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Dashboard

📊 **Overview**  
This project presents a Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Dashboard built using **Power BI**.  
The dataset was prepared in **Excel** and covers marketing performance across multiple channels, products, and regions over time.  

The dashboard provides insights into revenue trends, ROI, spend efficiency, and product-level contributions, enabling better marketing strategy decisions.

---

📂 **Dataset**  
Source: Internal marketing dataset (Excel)  
Key Features:  
- Date range: 01-01-2023 to 24-11-2024  
- Product-level revenue and conversions  
- Marketing channel performance (Email, Social Media, Search Engine, Direct Mail)  
- Regional breakdown (East, West, North, South)  
- Funnel metrics: clicks, impressions, spend, CPC, CTR, ROI  

---

🛠️ **Tools Used**  
- **Power BI**: For dashboard creation, interactive visuals, and data modeling  
- **Excel**: For dataset preparation, cleaning, and initial analysis  

---

📈 **Dashboard Features**  
**KPIs:**  
- Revenue: 1.04M  
- Total Clicks: 56K  
- Average CPC: 6.57  
- Total Spend: 0.26M  
- Average ROI: 4.48  
- Average CTR: 13.86%  

**Visualizations:**  
- Revenue by Channel and Quarter (Bar Chart)  
- Revenue by Product (Donut Chart)  
- Revenue vs Spend by Channel (Bar Chart)  
- Spend & ROI by Region (Bar Chart)  
- Revenue Trends by Quarter and Region (Line Chart)  
- Region-wise Product Performance (Table)  

---

🎯 **Insights**  
- Product **C** contributed the highest revenue share (33.11%).  
- Email and Social Media channels generated the strongest returns (~0.30M each).  
- The **East region** achieved the highest ROI with relatively low spend.  
- South region underperformed compared to others, highlighting an opportunity for optimization.  

---

🚀 **How to Use**  
1. Clone this repository.  
2. Open the `.pbix` file in **Power BI Desktop**.  
3. Connect to the dataset (Excel source).  
4. Explore the interactive dashboard using filters for **date, product, and channel**.  

---

📌 **Conclusion**  
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of marketing funnel performance, enabling better understanding of:  
- Channel efficiency  
- Product-level contributions  
- Regional ROI differences  
- Overall marketing spend optimization  

It serves as a valuable tool for marketing analysis and decision-making.
